ICBC and WorkSafeBC

Our clinics provide quick and convenient access to comprehensive care for patients covered by ICBC and WorkSafeBC. Our dedicated healthcare providers are committed to supporting your recovery and helping you regain your health and well-being.

Your ICBC and WorkSafeBC Questions Answered

What does your ICBC service include?

ICBC covers various medical services for patients injured in motor vehicle accidents, including medical assessments, physiotherapy, chiropractic care, and rehabilitation services. Our clinics aim to provide comprehensive care to support your recovery journey. 

What should I expect during my ICBC appointment?

During your initial appointment, you can expect a thorough assessment of your injuries by our healthcare providers. They will discuss your medical history, evaluate your condition, and develop a personalized treatment plan to aid your recovery. We prioritize your comfort and ensure you understand the steps involved in your care. 

What does your WorkSafeBC service include?

WorkSafeBC covers a range of medical services for workers who have been injured on the job, including medical assessments, treatment plans, rehabilitation services, and ongoing support for your recovery. Our clinics provide the care you need to help you return to work safely. How do I find an ICBC & WorkSafeBC clinic near me?  

What should I expect during my WorkSafeBC appointment?

During your appointment, you can expect a comprehensive assessment of your work-related injury by our healthcare providers. They will review your medical history, evaluate your condition, and discuss a personalized treatment plan tailored to your recovery needs. We aim to ensure you feel supported and informed throughout the process.
